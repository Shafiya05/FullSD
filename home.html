About EVConnect

EVConnect is a smart platform designed to help electric vehicle users easily locate nearby charging stations, check slot availability, and book charging sessions in advance.

As the adoption of electric vehicles continues to grow, EVConnect aims to provide a seamless and reliable charging experience through a simple and user-friendly system.

Our mission is to make EV charging convenient, accessible, and efficient for everyone while supporting a greener and more sustainable future.
